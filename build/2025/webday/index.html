When & Where?

December 20th 2025, 1:00PM until 5:30PM (add to your google calendar) @ Albina Library, Community Room 2A (directions)

Who & What?

Building on the previous event, Web Day will be two parts. First, we will have a series of group activities to explore the web together and learn about how it's connected. Later, we will transition into a Personal Website Workshop where everyone can work towards a slice of the web that they own and are proud of, whatever stage they might be at. Come for as much or as little as you can, it will all be fun.

Are you a beginner just starting out? Don't even have a website yet? Not sure how to find cool websites which aren't on social media? Perfect, this event is for you!

Are you a website enthusiast like us? Come hang out and help your community. This workshop is an unaffiliated, non-commercial community event.

Bring your laptop, or check one out from the library when you get here! The event is not affiliated with the library, but we are grateful to Albina Library for the use of their community room and resources like laptops!

Have a question? Not sure the event is for you? Email me, Reed, the host, at reed with 2 es at gmail.com (remove the spaces).
